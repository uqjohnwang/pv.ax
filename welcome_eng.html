Do you also feel that your online behavior is being watched all the time?


In fact, you can protect your personal privacy in the cyber world if you have not compromised.


The basic ways to help you protect your privacy are (if it is legally permitted):

Use a trusted VPN
Use incognito mode on browser
Use cloud host or Virtual Private Server
Use cryptocurrency
Use nickname on social platforms
Avoid uploading selfies on social platforms
Avoid associating work or school email with social platforms
Avoid using e-mailbox that has been hosted by big companies
Fill in inaccurate personal information
Advanced methods

Do you think it is troublesome to protect privacy?
Yes, protecting privacy does require extra time.
