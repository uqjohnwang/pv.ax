But in fact, when you want to circumvent government regulation,
there are more organizations trying to get your personal privacy,
because in their logic, behind the behavior of trying to evade government regulation,
it may be the gray industry, and this kind of business is easier to earn money for them.

The government does not only supervise citizens' privacy.
The government also regulates citizens' privacy while also enacting laws to protect citizens' privacy.
In most government supervision systems, there has been continuous efforts to prevent privacy leakage.

At the same time, the government's supervision of citizens' online behavior can also prevent crimes of stealing citizens' privacy.

As a consequence, we need to pay efforts on resolving conflicts between regulated dissatisfaction and potential risks of lack of supervision.
