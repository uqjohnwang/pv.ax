(please follow local laws to apply these methods):
Use multiple devices to log in to different social platforms
(not only Facebook, Snapchat, Twitter etc, you can even communicate through cyber games),
and contact friends who also use multiple devices to log in to different social platforms.
When encountering sensitive topics involving privacy,
disassemble the information into small pieces of text that cannot contain privacy and send them using different platforms.
Certain online platforms must verify the mobile phone number or email address to register and use,
but if you only want to use it for a short period of time,
you do not need to provide your own mobile phone number or email address.
You can use the receiving SMS or email service by online agency.
Combining different methods will be more effective, for example:
Use VPN to access cloud hosts, and then use VPN to browse different social platforms on the cloud host.
